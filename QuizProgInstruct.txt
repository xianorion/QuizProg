%a text file that open in Quiz prog to show the user how the format of the there text must be uploaded to work with QuizProg

Make sure your text file is in the following format:

1. place you questions after a number and a period. Then place your answers after the question
	on the following lines (Do not number the answers, QuizProg will do it for you based on order input)
>1.How many hours are in a day?
>	24
>	23
>	19

In the example above, Quizprog will set the questions as: "How many hours are in a day?" (anything after number+.)
Then it will assign a.24, b.23
